Scrum Artist Daily/Weekly Path

1) Daily: Self-tape using Quick Character Build (3-Step DT + Chubbuck).

2) Weekly: Join Storyboard Jam (5-Step DT), Acting Sprint (character deep dive), share all discoveries.

3) Biweekly: Participate in Vision Alignment Review to align personal and group goals.

4) Result: Feedback and progress are visible and cumulative.

  • Define: Story/scene goal
  • Ideate: Brainstorm new plot
  • Prototype: Try scenes
  • Test: Review, feedback, iterate
