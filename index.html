Manifesto: Why This Ensemble?

The Virtual Acting Ensemble Workflow reinvents digital-located theatre. We fuse structure (Agile/Scrum backbone), co-creation (Devised), social vision (Applied Theatre), creative iteration (Design Thinking), and deep character work (Chubbuck). Every tool and milestone comes from a real discipline—no generic steps. The mission: a modular, transparent, and sustainable process giving every artist creative agency, every team clarity, and every project true engagement—wherever, whenever.

Decentralized Authority, Unique Artist Producement. Every voice matters. Cybernetic, innovative, collective, and bold.

The Scrum Artist Rhythm

Daily: Individual character work + team standup
Weekly: Storyboard Jam + Training Wins sharing
Bi-weekly: Community Impact Review

This creates a sustainable rhythm where individual growth feeds collective creativity, and social purpose guides artistic development.
